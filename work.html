What is TS Eye?

We are very proud of our bespoke data solution software

By using the freshest possible data sourced from the retailers directly from the market, GMT Eye is able to identify any issues in sales, stock or promotional performance, producing an accurate and easy-to-read report on each store’s performance.

Your data feed is assembled and monitored by our in-house team Business Insight team, enabling us to identify and quickly respond to any dips in sales or underperforming products. With this GMT Eye allows us to minimize zero selling and dodge lost sales – all whilst maximising promotional activity.

Our own purposefully built, in store data capture and sales enabling software – built on the most secure, adapted and developed platforms, the application is loaded onto our Hardwar as standard and enables our Territory Team the evaluation of opportunities when on store visits.

The software itself breaks down the targeted goals for each team member at each individual store visit. Thanks to his portability, each team member can identify, influence and implement sales opportunities as they work their way around the store. Not only does GMT Eye make the field teams job quicker and easier, but it also swiftly implements information and content created by the teams at GMT. This includes economical and efficient journey plans to your store and work plan – keeping your coverage in touch with your guidance at all time.

What are the features of GMT Eye?

GMT eye facilitates the communication process, eliminating timely processes and to boost the availability & sales

Each Territorial Manager has their own unique username and password login for GMT Eye, ensuring all security protocols are met. Once logged in, they can navigate easily to each are of the application; from the CRM, detailing specific store information – including;

Location

Contact details

Personalized selling tips

Store attributes

History of previous Status: Availability, Presence, Prices & Sellout

They will also be able to review a log of current and previous visits – including records of actions implemented, actions required for current or future visits.

The Hardware carefully controls mandatory requirements of each visit including the necessity to insert before and after images, and the back-office management team support each request by preloading a list of tasks objectives and sales supporting aids for sales promoters. The application also includes a ‘live’ KPI league table, where each field force can a view their status of KPI score achieved, measured against their peers in the team.
